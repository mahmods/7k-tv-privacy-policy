Privacy Policy for 7k TV

Last Updated: November 27, 2024

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at 7k TV (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle any information we collect when you use our mobile application, 7k TV (“App”).

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to assure you that 7k TV does not collect any personal information from you. Specifically:

  • No Third-Party Services: Our App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media platforms.
  • No User Accounts: You do not need to create an account, register, or log in to use our App.
  • No In-App Purchases: Our App does not offer any in-app purchases or subscriptions.

2. Information We Collect

While we do not collect personal information, 7k TV does store certain non-personal data locally on your device to enhance your user experience:

  • Followed Streams: The App stores a list of streams you choose to follow. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

3. Use of Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no use of such data. The locally stored list of followed streams is used solely to provide you with a personalized experience within the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: The information stored on your device (i.e., the list of followed streams) is kept securely on your device and is not shared with any third parties.
  • Security Measures: Although we do not collect personal data, we implement reasonable security measures to protect any information stored locally on your device from unauthorized access.

5. Future Features

Push Notifications: While we do not currently send push notifications, we may introduce features such as "stream went live" notifications in the future. Should this feature be added, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and seek any necessary permissions from you.

6. Children’s Privacy

7k TV is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
